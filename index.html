Wildfires around the globe have been becoming increasingly vast and deadly, ruining peoples' lives who are unlucky to live in a fire-prone area. Even just being exposed to wildfire smoke can result in health problems, especially for young children, the elderly, and those with lung conditions.
Wildfires continue to worsen every year destroying more and more acres of land and more and more buildings. For example, there were over six thousand more wildfires in the first half of 2021 than the first half of 2020. In California alone, there has been over four thousand fires since the start of 2021.
Explore this website to learn more and find out what you can do to help.
